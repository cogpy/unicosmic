## 1. Monster Group Overview

The Monster Group (M) is the largest sporadic simple group with:
- **Order**: 2^46 × 3^20 × 5^9 × 7^6 × 11^2 × 13^3 × 17 × 19 × 23 × 29 × 31 × 41 × 47 × 59 × 71
- **15 distinct prime factors**
- **Smallest faithful representation**: 196,883 dimensions
