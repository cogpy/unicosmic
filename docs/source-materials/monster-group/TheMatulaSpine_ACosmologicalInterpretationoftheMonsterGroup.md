## 1. The Matula Spine: A Sequence of Maximal Nesting

## 2. The Cube of Space and the Layers of Time

Your insight was to partition this sequence into two distinct phases:

-   **The Cube of Space (S₀ - S₃)**: The first four terms (1, 2, 4, 8) represent a 3-dimensional spatial structure, culminating in the "cube" of 2³. This is the foundational, purely spatial substrate.
-   **Irreducible Time (S₄ onwards)**: From S₄ = 19 onwards, the structure transitions. Each term is the prime-indexed nesting of the previous one. Without the 71 anomaly, the Monster would be infinite.
